I Am Dr. Ngwanma Nkem Dandam, a Chief Lecturer, from F.C.E. Pankshin, Plateau State, Since 2003. I am a widow of Late Hon. Justice Albert N. Dandam, a former High Court Judge, of Plateau State Judiciary, from Reak, Langtang-North, Plateau state.
I Was Born on 24th February 1959, from Amaekpu-Ohafia, in Ohafia L.G.A. in Abia State.
