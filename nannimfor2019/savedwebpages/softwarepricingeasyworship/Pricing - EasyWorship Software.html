Frequently Asked Questions
How many computers does my license cover?
With the EasyWorship site license our software can be installed on unlimited computers, as long as they are associated with a single campus. Each additional campus will need its own license.
What’s your refund policy?
If you aren’t happy with your EasyWorship purchase, just let us know within 90 days and we will refund 100% of your money. We are confident in our product and we want you to share that confidence.
Waiting on budget approval?
We get it. Every church has its own unique purchasing process, and sometimes that means you have to wait. Don't let the wait keep you from experiencing EasyWorship! Contact us today at orders@easyworship.com and see what EasyWorship can do for you.
Do you offer bulk discounts for my denomination or church fellowship?
Yes we do, as long as the churches in your group are purchasing the software at the same time. Please contact our team directly at orders@easyworship.com for more details.
My church used to have EasyWorship but now we use a different product. Do we still qualify for the upgrade price?
Absolutely. If your church has owned EasyWorship at any point -- even if you’ve switched to another product -- you qualify for the upgrade price.
Can I try EasyWorship before I buy it?
You bet. Download the demo of EasyWorship to try out all our features and even build schedules and presentations for use within a registered copy. Demo use limited by a watermark on live output. Try EasyWorship
