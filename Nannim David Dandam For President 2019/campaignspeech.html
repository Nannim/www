  Major Agenda Points

1. Enhance our Agriculture by employing our indigenous manufacturers for large scale automated mechanised farming to ensure that food is in good supply and exportable.

2. Enhance our Construction by employing our indigenous manufacturers for construction machinery at a natural cost to help our construction and export some of the machines.

3. Enhance our Healthcare; the presence of good food and construction industries provide the means to have ultra-modern healthcare facilities and medicinal plants synthesised for medicine production.

4. Enhance/Reform our educational curricul        ums to enable students/pupils learn what really needs to be known, early enough.

5. Enhance our energy sector by looking to alternative sources of energy.
